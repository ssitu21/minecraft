Overcoming weakness


Change your practicing habits if you feel stuck.


    If you are stuck, change the way u practice. Focus on your weaknesses and try new exercises to improve.

Evaluate your improvement by a new measurement.


    Try measuring your progress in a new way. Focus on different skills, like accuracy or technique.

Dont give up


    Never give up if a skill is hard,practice daily and focus on what you’re doing well.Mistakes are part of learning—they help you grow and avoid future errors.
