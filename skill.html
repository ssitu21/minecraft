Learning skills


Reduce the number of distractions while you're learning:


    Learn better by avoiding distractions. Find a quiet spot, focus on one task. Use instrumental music if you want and keep your phone away to stay focused.

Break learning the task into parts:


    Break learning into smaller parts. Focus on one step or topic at a time.

Connect the information to something you understand:


    Relate new information to something you already know.
